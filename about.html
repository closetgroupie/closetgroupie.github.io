About Sewingly

Sewingly is a peer to peer marketplace for fabric and needle artists. We invite sewists, knitters, weavers, crocheters, smockers, and any other type of needle artist to sell their work through Sewingly. We also welcome specialty craft supply shops to sell their merchandise on Sewingly.
